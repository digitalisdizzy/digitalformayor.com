Why Digital?

LEO Reform

Law Enforcement (like SSO and SFPD) will be reformed to make it fairer for citizens, hold officers accountable, and reduce the crime rate.

Accessible Legal System

The legal system will be made easier, clearer, and more accessible to citizens, so that everybody's voice is heard.

Democracy and Transparency

There will be frequent council meetings open to all, where your voice will be heard. Freedom of Information Act requests will be available for all.
